Our Story

Where West
Meets Seoul

Nestled at the heart of Orchard Road, Cafe de Muse is Singapore's only 24-hour Western-Korean fusion café. From the first espresso of the morning to the last bite of a midnight dessert, we're here for every moment.

Every dish is crafted with care and passion — thoughtfully designed to satisfy your cravings. We constantly refine our craft, bringing out the best taste to excite your palate.
